ELEVATION · NEON GLOW, NOT DROP-SHADOW

Glow & Elevation

There is almost no soft drop-shadow in this system. "Raised" reads as a hairline border or a neon glow keyed to the accent. The only true shadow is for overlays (dropdowns / modals).

// Rule

Cards and panels never carry a drop-shadow at rest. On hover, a card brightens its border to the section accent and slides a 2px accent bar in on the left — see components-cards.html. Reserve glow for the one element that should pull the eye.
