Stacks live in colors_and_type.css + tokens/tokens.json (drift-check asserts they match, and the fallbacks export to every native platform). RTL mirror block: components.css §13. Fallback families (Noto Sans Thai / Arabic / CJK SC) are system/Google faces — not shipped here — so rendering depends on the platform having them; Apple platforms also cover these via system-ui.
